Kim Mingyu | SEVENTEEN

The All-Rounder of SEVENTEEN ✨

Kim Mingyu (김민규) is a South Korean singer, rapper, and visual of the 13-member boy group SEVENTEEN under Pledis Entertainment. Known as the "All-Rounder" of the group, Mingyu excels in dancing, rapping, singing, and even composing!

  • Zodiac Sign: Aries
  • Height: 187 cm (6'2")
  • Nationality: Korean
  • Position: Rapper, Vocalist, Visual

Personality & Traits 🐶

Mingyu is known for his bright and energetic personality. Despite his intimidating visuals and height, he's actually very sweet and caring towards members and fans alike. He's often called the "Golden Retriever" of SEVENTEEN because of his loyal and playful nature.

Some special things about Mingyu:

  • Has a famous "Mingyu Bread" incident where he burned bread during a live broadcast
  • Known for his incredible cooking skills (except when it comes to bread!)
  • Has a dog named Aengdu (which means "grape" in Korean)
  • Loves photography and often shares his photos
  • Has the "Golden Ratio" face that matches Korean beauty standards perfectly

Career Highlights 🎤

Mingyu debuted with SEVENTEEN on May 26, 2015 with the mini album "17 Carat". Since then, he's been involved in:

  • Songwriting and composition for SEVENTEEN's albums
  • MC for various music shows
  • Appeared on numerous variety shows showcasing his charming personality
  • Modeling for brands like Calvin Klein Korea
  • Photography exhibitions showcasing his work
